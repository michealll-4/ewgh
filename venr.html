Why rest matters
Slow-wave sleep replays new memories in the neocortex (Stickgold, 2005). Testing yourself and sleeping directly after produces 35% better recall the next day than cramming without sleep (Mazza et al., 2016). The forgetting curve is steepest in the first hour — rest now, review tomorrow.
